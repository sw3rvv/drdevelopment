About Us

D & R Development Solutions Corp. is a family owned and operated general contracting company with decades of experience in the construction sector. We are fully insured and licensed within our industry and located in the Greater Toronto Area.

Our team can successfully execute both renovations and new builds in the residential and commercial sectors. We are proficient in completing projects on time, with the highest level of craftsmanship, while remaining cost-effective.
